Effect of Third-Party Airstrikes on Rebel Activity: Evidence from Yemen

I am currently working on my master's thesis which will examine the effect of the Saudi-led coalition airstrikes in Yemen on Houthi activity. While previous studies have sought to test similar effects in other cases using geographic matching methods, I find theoretical issues with this approach and offer a potential alternative.
